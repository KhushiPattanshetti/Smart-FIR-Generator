न्याय सेतु

Bridge of Justice

Welcome to Nyaya Setu, an official application of the Indian Police Service developed to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of First Information Report (FIR) registration.


This application addresses the challenge of unavailability of legal experts at police stations by providing intelligent assistance in determining the appropriate sections and acts to be included in FIRs, thereby reducing errors and improving subsequent investigation processes.
